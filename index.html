About

Aaron Bergh's infatuation with brewing began during his time at Cal Poly as a Plant Science major. Through the Learn by Doing philosophy, Bergh has been able to arise as one of the youngest distillers on the West Coast. He credits much of his success back to Cal Poly and the foundation that his college education set for him going forward.

Bergh's business, Calwise Spririts, is famous for its renowned tastes made with authentic ingredients inspired by local scenery from the California Coastline. Bergh's mission is to capture the beauty of California through his spirits. He yearns to capture the essence of nature through Calwise, and share that experience with his customers.

Cal Poly to Calwise

Cal Poly to Calwise

From the Cal Poly dorm rooms to now, Bergh has made a name for himself on the Central Coast. Originally starting in the Hothouse shortly after graduation, Bergh recently moved to the center of spirits world -- Tin City (Paso Robles, CA).
